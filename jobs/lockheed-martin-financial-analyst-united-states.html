About the Role

The Financial Analyst position at Lockheed Martin is a full-time opportunity designed for professionals who thrive in fast-paced, results-oriented environments. As a Financial Analyst, you will play a pivotal role in supporting Lockheed Martins strategic objectives while working alongside a team of dedicated and highly skilled individuals. This role offers unparalleled exposure to industry-leading practices, cutting-edge tools, and a collaborative work culture that encourages innovation at every level.

In this role, you will be responsible for driving key initiatives, managing critical workflows, and delivering measurable outcomes that align with Lockheed Martins long-term vision. Whether you are collaborating with cross-functional teams, engaging with stakeholders, or independently managing complex projects, your work as a Financial Analyst will directly contribute to the organization continued growth and success.

Key Responsibilities

As a Financial Analyst at Lockheed Martin, your day-to-day responsibilities will include:

  • Lead and manage core Financial Analyst functions across Lockheed Martins United States operations, ensuring all activities align with organizational goals and quality standards

  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams including product, engineering, marketing, and operations to deliver integrated solutions and achieve shared business objectives

  • Develop, implement, and continuously improve processes, systems, and workflows that enhance productivity and operational efficiency across all relevant departments

  • Analyze data, generate insights, and present findings to senior leadership and key stakeholders to support informed decision-making and strategic planning

  • Build and maintain strong relationships with internal teams, external partners, clients, and vendors to ensure seamless communication and successful project delivery

  • Monitor industry trends, competitive landscapes, and emerging best practices to ensure Lockheed Martin remains at the forefront of its field

  • Mentor and support junior team members, providing guidance, feedback, and coaching to help them grow professionally

  • Prepare detailed reports, documentation, presentations, and strategic plans that communicate progress and recommendations clearly and concisely

  • Manage multiple projects simultaneously, prioritizing tasks effectively and meeting deadlines without compromising on quality

  • Participate actively in team meetings, planning sessions, and company-wide initiatives, contributing ideas that help shape Lockheed Martins direction

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor degree or higher in Business Administration, Computer Science, Engineering, Marketing, Finance, or a related discipline

  • A minimum of 3 to 5 years of professional experience in a Financial Analyst role or closely related position

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to interpret complex data and develop actionable strategies

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English

  • Proficiency with industry-standard tools and software relevant to the Financial Analyst function

  • Proven ability to work independently and as part of a team, managing time effectively

  • Strong organizational skills and meticulous attention to detail

Preferred Qualifications

  • Advanced degree such as a Masters, MBA, or professional certification relevant to the Financial Analyst function

  • Previous experience working in a United States-based or international setting

  • A passion for continuous learning and staying current with the latest trends in the Financial Analyst field
